Authencity

Reviews must be genuine and based on personal experiences. Fabricated or biased reviews will not be tolerated.

Respectfulness

All reviews and comments must be respectful towards individuals, including dining hall staff, other reviewers, and fellow students.

Relevance

Reviews should focus on the dining hall experience, including food quality, variety, cleanliness, service, and overall atmosphere. Off-topic or irrelevant content will be removed.

Accuracy

Reviewers are encouraged to provide accurate information about their experiences, including specific details about the dishes tried, wait times, pricing, and any notable aspects of the dining hall.

Constructiveness

Reviews should aim to be constructive, providing helpful feedback for both the dining hall management and potential visitors. Criticism should be offered in a constructive manner.

No Discrimination

Discriminatory language or content based on factors such as race, gender, religion, sexual orientation, or disability will not be tolerated.

No Personal Attacks

Personal attacks against individuals, including staff members or other reviewers, are strictly prohibited.

No Promotion or Spam

Reviews should not be used for promotional purposes or to advertise businesses, products, or services. Additionally, spammy content, including repeated postings or irrelevant links, will be removed.

Original Content

Reviews must be original and not copied from other sources. Plagiarism will result in the removal of the review.

Privacy

Reviewers should respect the privacy of individuals and avoid sharing personal information, such as names or contact details, without consent.

Moderation

The review page will be moderated to ensure compliance with these rules. Reviews that violate these guidelines may be edited or removed at the discretion of the moderators.

Feedback

Users are encouraged to provide feedback on the dining hall review page itself, including suggestions for improvement or reporting any issues with the platform.


By adhering to these rules, the review page aims to provide a valuable resource for students and visitors seeking information about college campus dining halls.
