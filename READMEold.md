## Name
RateMyDiningHall

## Description
Welcome to RateMyDiningHall, the ultimate platform for college students to rate and review their campus dining halls. Whether you're looking for the best spot to grab a bite or want to share your latest dining experience, RateMyDiningHall has got you covered.

## Visuals
[Home Page](https://imgur.com/a/vqhancp)
[Rating Page](https://imgur.com/ErF1qCU)


## Features
- User Reviews and Ratings: Share your thoughts on dining halls and rate them on a scale of 1 to 5 stars.
- Browse Dining Halls: Explore dining halls from various colleges and universities.
- Search Functionality: Find specific dining halls by name filters.
